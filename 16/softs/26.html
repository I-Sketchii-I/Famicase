Blastressa

Petite Panboo / design student

“Blastressa” is not only the title of this game but also the name of the main character herself. The 8 year old Tressa was the first-and-only-born of a royal dynasty and never had to ask twice for anything. But she grew tired of abundance soon and decided to fight to bring peace to the world since this was one of the rare things her parents could not buy her. “Blastressa” is a first Person shooter set in a world whose appearance is strongly influenced by the imagination of a 8-year old girl. You fight with cute weapons like a broccoli canon and use practical tools like your strawberry bubblegum to navigate through the levels.
