Night of the Living Cartridge

Mateusz Czech / Graphic Designer

Do you think playing Famicom is totally safe? Watch out! Inserting this cartridge will change your life forever. Inanimate objects come to life causing havoc all over the place. The only one who can stop them is you. New game from Beri Studio offers fearsome moments in alternate world, where electronic demons and other monstrosities come to life after the dusk. Stand up, fight, and save the world before it’s too late.
