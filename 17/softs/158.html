激走!! ナイン・ライフ

Stu Brett / GHOSTARCA.DE

2068. Champion motorcycle racer, Sol Reznor is shot and left for dead by an organized crime syndicate, intent on corrupting the sport. Nine months later and awaking from a coma, Sol finds himself attached to a life-support unit. Sol finds a way to plan his revenge by fitting his life support machine into an armored motorcycle. With the aid of his his C.A.T (Cerebal Articulation Terminal), Sol can remotely fire his bike's machine guns. It is a suicide mission. Sol must wipe out the nine leaders of the syndicate and destroy their army of thugs.
