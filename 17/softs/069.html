リリス 愛のサクリファイス

Ruby / Designer & Illustrator

You play as Amarenzu, a lone wolf who stumbled upon an ancient goddess named Lilitu. Lilitu promises to grant your any desire if you can prove to her how much you love her by bringing her sacrifices. Travel the ancient world to find what Lilitu desires~ But as you progress, Lilitu's requests become more difficult! Can you make Lilitu happy?
